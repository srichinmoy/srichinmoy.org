Original Selected Links

A selection of related sites and articles about Sri Chinmoy. For more information on Sri Chinmoy and his works, kindly peruse the following sites:

Sri Chinmoy Library

Sri Chinmoy Library is an online catalogue for Sri Chinmoy’s  collected works of over 1500 books. These include books on poetry, philosophy, and spiritual talks. Sri Chinmoy has also written many plays and essays on spiritual and cultural figures

Sri Chinmoy Centre

The Sri Chinmoy Centre exists to provide an opportunity for students of Sri Chinmoy to meet on a regular basis for meditation and other types of activities. With over 300 Centres around the world, the Sri Chinmoy Centre is truly a family of all races, cultures and religions. On the Sri Chinmoy Centre site you can view articles, blogs and photos by Sri Chinmoy Centre Members.

Sri Chinmoy TV

Sri Chinmoy TV is an online resource for videos about Sri Chinmoy and the Sri Chinmoy Centre. These include videos of Sri Chinmoy in meditation and extracts of Sri Chinmoy’s latest music concerts. Sri Chinmoy TV is also home to the popular podcast of Inspiration News. Presented by students of Sri Chinmoy, Inspiration News offers a selection of “good news from around the world.”

Radio Sri Chinmoy

Radio Sri Chinmoy offers a wide variety of meditative music, including recordings of Maestro Sri Chinmoy playing on numerous different musical instruments. Radio Sri Chinmoy also hosts “Inspiration Sounds” a podcast of stories written by Sri Chinmoy and read out for audio recordings.

Sri Chinmoy Songs

This is an online library for over 17,000 of Sri Chinmoy’s songs. Primarily devotional in nature these songs are based on Sri Chinmoy's direct experiences of God and the spiritual life. This is a valuable resource for those who wish to enter into the world of Sri Chinmoy’s music and songs.

Sri Chinmoy Poetry

A selection of Sri Chinmoy’s poetry. Includes both short inspirational aphorisms and longer poems from Sri Chinmoy’s early years. There are also collections of poems into different poetic themes.

Sri Chinmoy Races

The Sri Chinmoy Marathon Team organises many races around the world. These include short 2 mile races, to the world's longest certified race of 3100 miles. The Sri Chinmoy races site provides results and information on upcoming events.

Sri Chinmoy Art

An introduction to the beautiful world of Sri Chinmoy’s Jharna Kala art. Jharna Kala or “Fountain Art” is the name Sri Chinmoy has given to his mystical paintings which stem from his own inner source of creativity and spontaneity. Sri Chinmoy has composed over 150,000 Jharna Kala paintings and over 13 million Soul Birds.

World Harmony Run

Sri Chinmoy is the founder and inspiration behind the global world harmony run. This relay run is held in 6 different continents and touches the lives of many people. The World Harmony run seeks to inspire people to live together in greater harmony, understanding and friendship

Sri Chinmoy Inspiration

The Sri Chinmoy Inspiration group is a site where people are welcome to offer news, inspiration, encouragement and humorous stories. Both students and friends of Sri Chinmoy are warmly invited to share their views and experiences.

Sri Chinmoy Books

Offers the opportunity to preview and purchase many of Sri Chinmoy’s most popular books. Includes best sellers such as Meditation, Wings of Joy and the recent release “Sri Chinmoy’s Heart Garden”.
