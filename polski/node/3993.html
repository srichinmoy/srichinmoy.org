Kind words from religious and spiritual figures

“This meeting of ours has been most essential. Your message and mine are the same. When we both leave this world, you and I, we will meet together.”

- Pope Paul VI, (March 22nd, 1972)

“You are part of the spiritual force of love that emanates from God and which will transform the evil of this world into its counterpart. Thank you for persisting and going on, going on. Perhaps the world continues in existence only because of people like yourselves who help to hold it in being.”

- Archbishop Desmond Tutu (March 16th, 2004)

"I have always treasured Sri Chinmoy in my heart and soul, and often talk about him at my meetings because it is a very special, rare thing to find a genuine representative of spirituality. This is what we feel in the presence of Sri Chinmoy—authenticity."

- Pir Vilayat Khan (Apr. 23rd, 1989)
Head of the Sufi Order in the West.

"The world badly needs more peaceful and loving men like Sri Chinmoy. I am a Muslim and he is serving all people of peace. Sri Chinmoy is doing so much for the world, for people of all faiths, because of his own peace and faith."

- Muhammad Ali,
Three-time World Heavyweight Boxing Champion.

"Sri Chinmoy and I are part of so many religious leaders who believe that we are trying to usher in a new age, an age where all religions can talk about one God.

- Monsignor Tom Hartman
Archdiocese of Rockville Center, Long Island, New York. Presenter of The God Squad' 1

"Sri Chinmoy, your own life's work of most devoted service to interfaith understanding and world-oneness is unparalleled."

- Rev. James Park Morton
Chairman & Organiser of the Annual Interfaith Service, President of the Interfaith Center of New York

"Sri Chinmoy is trying to bring down peace to the entire world... Whereever he goes, he (Sri Chinmoy) is a direct sign of peace for all who see him. He can bring all the nations and the different cultures into one. To me the path of oneness and loving mercy of Sri Chinmoy is fully and totally one with the path of Lord Buddha. Sri Chinmoy is simply on our tiny earth here and now to share the same loving kindnes, mercy and spiritual power."

- Phra Kru Vinaithorn Virasak Kittivaro
Venerable Abbot of Khao Takiab Temple, Hua Hin, Thailand 2

"Sri Chinmoy will be a great hope of Buddhism to bring the message of the Lord Buddha to the people of the world."

- Venerable Pratamkunaporn Abbot of Wat Pho
Royal Temple of Thailand, December 19th 1988 3

"The spirit of dedication with which Sri Chinmoy has been devoting his energies in lifting the hearts of men and women into spiritual realms of prayer and meditation for world peace is highly praiseworthy."

- Professor Harmindar Singh
Vice Chairman, World Conference on Religions and Peace 4
