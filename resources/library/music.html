Music Recordings

Sri Chinmoy is a prolific composer with thousands of songs to his credit in his native Bengali and English. He plays many instruments from all over the world including a variety of flutes, the Indian esraj, cello, harmonium, piano and pipe organ.

To listen to music recordings of Sri Chinmoy, kindly visit Radio Sri Chinmoy
